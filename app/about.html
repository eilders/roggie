About

While you’re reviewing the benefits of the Roggie Water Conditioner, you will realize that it is quite simply better water at every faucet in your home.

The System reduces the surface tension of the water which makes it feel softer and improves its cleaning effectiveness.

It improves the efficiency of all the detergents, fabric softeners, lawn, pool, and spa chemicals, as well as fertilizers we would use. This allows you to further reduce your usage of those products which save money and the environment while continuing to use the same amounts and see improved results from them. You may not cut back on your laundry products, but your laundry is cleaner, brighter, and softer.

The changes and improvements that will impress you the most are as follows:

  • Your hair is softer and more manageable.
  • Your shower and tub are so much easier to clean. It melts away stuff that Comet and Tilex wouldn’t touch.
  • Your water tastes so much better.
  • Your hot tub water is more forgiving of neglect. The water stays clearer and doesn’t smell funky. You are able to use fewer chemicals and still have clean, clearer water.
  • Your laundry is cleaner, brighter, and softer to the touch.
  • Your plants and garden are greener and stronger from using electro-magnetized water.

However, most people don’t cut back on chemical/detergent usage; they just get better performance from what they do use. So, there are “quality of life” issues more than money saving issues.

The main payback comes from energy, maintenance, and replacement cost savings.

Energy saving are realized by the prevention and removal of sacle build-up on a heat exchanger surface. Even a thin film (1/32″ or 0.8 mm) can increase energy consumption by nearly 10%.
