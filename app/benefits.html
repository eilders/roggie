The Roggie Water Conditioning Benefits For Your Home

If you are experiencing any of these problems:

  • Clogged water pipes
  • Water heater inefficiency and saling
  • Decreased water pressure
  • Frozen valves and faucets on water heaters and sink
  • Scale or sports on glass, silverware, pots, pans and plastics
  • Crust rings in toilet tanks and white spotting on bathtubs, tile and chrome
  • White deposits on showerheads
  • Hard to remove soap film on shower tiles, doors or curtains
  • Poor sudsing of soap or shampoo
  • White crust on the surface of potted plants

You are paying for poor water quality (higher electric bills, repairs, replacements, etc) whether you own a water softener or not.

Water Conditioners provide the following benefits:

  • Food – May reduce the amount of sugar, salt, spices, coffee or tea you normally use.
  • Health – For heart patients and those with hypertension, it does not add salt to food or water.
  • Skin Care – Improves your complexion and is gentler on your skin.
  • Clothing – Clothes last longer and come out looking cleaner while using less soap.
  • Appliances – Quality improvement in ice makers, coffee makers, dishwashers or any other appliance that uses water.
  • Glassware – Dishes and glasses come out sparkling clean without unsightly water spot all with using less dishwashing detergents.
  • Vegetation – Plants and grass grow quicker and healthier.
  • Automobiles – Reduce harmful water spotting when washing your car or truck.
  • Pools and Spas – Swim or relax in crystal clear water that require fewer chemicals, like clarifiers, purifiers, and chlorine, which saves you money and increases your comfort.
  • Water Heaters, Pipes, and Fixtures – Cleans out previous scale build-up while preventing new deposits from forming.

Environmental Advantages:

  • Reduces electric usage.
  • Reduces water usage.
  • Does not pollute ground water or interfere with septic systems.
  • Caustic cleaners become infrequent and unnecessary.
  • Top soils have a natural mineral content carried to plants.
  • Landfills will not have unnecessarily discarded toilet bowls, water heaters, dishwasher, washing machines, etc. Water-using devices, pipes and appliances retain like-new quality for decades without lime scale build-up.
  • Reduces need for digging, fracturing concrete slabs, tearing up streets, etc. due to pipe blockage.

Which would you rather do? Spend a fraction of the cost of a typical salt water softener or spend more time and money dealing with lime scale?
